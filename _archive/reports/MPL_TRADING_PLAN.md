- Tick size: 0.1 ($0.50 per tick)
- Point value: $5.00 per point
- Typical ORB size: 1-3 points

Example ($50k account):
- 0.50% risk = $250
- ORB size = 2.0 points = $100 risk per contract
- Position = $250 / $100 = 2-3 contracts
